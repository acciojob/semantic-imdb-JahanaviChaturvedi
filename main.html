Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, a thief who specializes in corporate espionage by infiltrating the subconscious of his targets. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for planting another person's idea into a target's subconscious. The film explores themes of dreams within dreams and the blurring of reality and imagination.
